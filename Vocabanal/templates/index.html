Are you a...?

Whatever you are non-native English scientific writer who produce papers, or an English teacher, you can use this website to analyze your document. You can produce a wordcloud image, or even visualize and download the distribution of the most occurrent parts of speech (nouns, verbs, adjectives, and adverbs). Additionally, it is possible to visualize the word count of the extracted keywords grouped by their lemmas.
